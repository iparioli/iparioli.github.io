Our Menu

The Pizzeria and coffe shop “ I Parioli” offers a wide variety of first courses, main courses, appetizers and side dishes based on meat, fish and vegetables; the ingredients used are selected by chefs with extensive experience and are fresh and genuine. It is possible to accompany the dishes with a rich selection of high quality of white and red wines, ideal to enhance the flavors, or with fresh and non-alcoholic drinks.

Do you want to reserve a table at “ I Parioli”? You can contact the restaurant and provide all the details about your reservation indicating the number of people, the day and the time.
